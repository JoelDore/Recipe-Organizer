Thank you for joining Recip-EZ™, your handy online recipe organizer!



To begin, click on the "Add New Recipe" button that has appeared on the side-bar to your left, this will take you to our EZ™ Recipe template. Fill in the neccessary information like the Title, where you got the recipe, give it a rating, and jot down some notes and PRESTO it's saved under your personal log in! To view previous saved recipes, either click on Categories or Sources, choose a dish, and you'll have all the recipe pop up; link and all!
